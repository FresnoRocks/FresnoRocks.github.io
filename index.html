The World Is Mine

I once tried to be Batman but I didn't have the money to build a Bat Cave or all the cool gadgets he uses. For that reason I decided to become a graphic designer. The world thrives on new ideas and I want to be the guy who makes those new ideas happen. Maybe it's a new app that helps people sell their shoes from home. Or maybe it's the website I build for the next big bakery. Even if it's just the logo for a new local restaurant, I'll be the one to make it happen.
