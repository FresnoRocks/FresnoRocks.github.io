The World Is Mine

I once tried to be batman, but i didn’t have a lot of money to build a batcave and all the cool toys he uses. And for that reason I decide to be a graphic designer. The world drives on new ideas and i want to be that guy that makes the new ideas that drives the world. If that is a new app to help people sell their shoes from their home, if it’s the next big bakery i build a website for or if it’s just a logo for a local restaurant..
